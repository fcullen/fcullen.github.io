[KLEVER](https://www.kicc.cam.ac.uk/projects/klever) (Kmos LEnsed Velocity and Emission line Review) is an ESO Large Programme targeting around 200 galaxies at 1 < z < 2.5 with [KMOS](https://www.eso.org/sci/facilities/paranal/instruments/kmos/overview.html) on the VLT.
Galaxies are observed in all three primary near-IR bands (J, H, K; covering ~ 1.0 to 2.5 &mu;m) in order to cover the full suite of rest-frame optical emission lines.
This dataset will enable a detailed, spatially-resolved, investigation of the physical properties of the ISM at the peak epoch of cosmic star formation.
